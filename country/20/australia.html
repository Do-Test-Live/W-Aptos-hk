Overview

Mara Aesthetics was founded by Mara Bontempo-Ross, an accredited medical aesthetic consultant with more than two decade’s experience in the medical and cosmetic industries.


Our business focus is to provide medical professionals with the highest quality scientifically researched products backed with accredited training to ensure superior cosmetic results.


Thread lifting is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that has become very popular internationally and is now well accepted in Australia and New Zealand. We offer clients the latest technology absorbable threads to provide a natural lift to enhancing the natural facial contours without scarring and with a minimum of downtime and importantly with long lasting results
