Overview

Mitocon Biomed is a pioneering company founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionise the aesthetic industry in India. The mission is to elevate the standards of aesthetic care in the country by introducing cutting-edge global technology and innovations.

Proudly partnering with renowned companies like Möller Medical from Germany, Sebbin Paris, and Hanson Medical from the USA, Mitocon Biomed is committed to providing top-notch solutions and transforming the landscape of aesthetics in India. 

Aptos is delighted to join Mitocon Biomed on their journey to bring world-class aesthetic threads to enhance the beauty and well-being of the Indians.
