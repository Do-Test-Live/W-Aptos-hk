Nasolabial Folds: Correction of the folds without fillers and scalpel

Nasolabial folds are best described as the two skin folds on the side of the nose and corner of the mouth. It is said that as a person ages, the fold becomes more pronounced, but not always. Nasolabial folds can also form in young people due to genetic predisposition or excessive facial expressions, weight loss and bad habits. As a result, problem appears and hardly is hidden by makeup.



To get rid of wrinkles with the help of massage is unlikely to succeed; moreover, an unprofessional procedure can worsen the situation by stretching new folds. Don’t risk, it is better to ask professionals for help. They can offer a more delicate and effective solution.


 Lots of people use HA fillers to correct the wrinkles on the face but this method can in some cases only worsen the situation, making the face "heavy." Besides, fillers sometimes are not enough and to improve results it is better to combine them with threads.


Correction of nasolabial folds with Aptos threads


Aptos has developed several methods to solve this problem:


Aptos Nano Spring (tiny threads) - absorbable spring-threads are designed for correction of mimic wrinkles. Nano line makes it possible to smooth wrinkles even on very delicate areas of the face. This is a very simple method and the results are immediately apparent. The procedure is fast and painless, and a special shape allows threads to follow the movements of the facial muscles, thus preserving natural facial expressions.


Light Lift Needle 2G, Light Lift Needle 2G Soft or Light Lift Needle 2G Blunt - the whole experience of the company against aging in one line. Absorbable threads are composed of polylactic acid, which stimulates rejuvenation processes at the cellular level. Once degradation process takes place under the skin, the microdoses of hydrolized substance provide an effect of mesotherapy for about 360 days. In addition, polylactic acid stimulates new collagen growth thus creating a flexible supporting capsule under the skin, which prevents from further ptosis for about two years.


Aptos Excellence Visage or Aptos Excellence Visage Soft can lift soft tissues and prevent from ptosis for 2-3 years, depending on the individual characteristics of the patient.



Aptos Wire - a more radical method of rejuvenation, designed to correct deep wrinkles, folds and scars on the face. Experts call it "wire scalpel" because of similar result. However, with Wire it is possible to pass the needle through hypodermic tissues on a closed contour without skin incisions. Wire separates skin and soft tissues only through pin punctures. Correction lasts about half an hour under local anesthesia. Unevenness of the skin is removed and only small punctures are visible immediately after the procedure. These inconveniences disappear a 2-3 days after treatment. One procedure using Aptos Wire is enough to completely get rid of nasolabial folds.


The method of correction is selected by a specialist individually.
