What to know for before consultation to doctor

The effectiveness of the thread lifting highly depends on how the consultation will take place: how much the specialist will inspire confidence in the patient, and the patient, in turn, will retell about the state of health, lifestyle and the presence of certain diseases.


Excitement is natural


Often because of the excitement during consultation, patients forget to clarify the questions they are interested in. The excitement in this situation is fully understandable and normal. Get rid of the possible fears and excitement will help preliminary conversation with the doctor, during which it is recommended to ask any exciting questions, even quite insignificant at first glance.

We recommend that you plan and write them in a calm environment. So, after a conversation with a doctor, you will not have any doubts about the expediency of carrying out a thread lifting and, in the future, during and after procedure, you will not be accompanied by excitement and uncomfortable feelings, possible occurrence of which you might learn about during the consultation.


Preparation of a package of references and documents


We recommend to prepare and come to the primary consultation in advance with a package of necessary documents: references, the results of examinations and tests that prove the presence or absence of diseases that may become a contraindication to the procedure of a thread lifting, the admission of special preparations, and, if necessary, recommendations of your doctor.


Contraindications to the implementation of thread modeling


It is very important before the procedure to inform the doctor about your general health, chronic, acute and hereditary diseases, recently had diseases. Among the contraindications:

·      Cardiac ischemia;

·      Arterial hypertension;

·      Diseases of the immune system;

·      Inflammatory processes and tumors in the field of procedure;

·      Blood diseases and coagulation disorders;

·      Skin diseases;

·      Mental and neurotic abnormalities;

·      Exacerbation of chronic diseases;

·      Presence of oncological diseases;

·      Pregnancy and lactation;

·      Tendency to form keloid scars after surgery;

·      Previously introduced non-biodegradable implants in the correction zone;

·      reception of anticoagulants and antiaggregants, non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.


Do not be afraid to clarify and ask again


If from a conversation with a specialist you could not understand certain points, do not be afraid to ask them again, because this will affect the results of the procedure and its successful conduct. All the prescribed drugs and medicines are needed for the normal course of the recovery processes, additionally write down their names with your handwriting, so that after some time you will not forget about what preparations you have been prescribed.

Do not forget that your condition during the rehabilitation period and the effectiveness of the procedure will depend directly on how accurately you will fulfill the instructions and comply with the requirements of doctors.


It is extremely important that before the procedure of the thread lifting the patient clearly thought out his expectations and described them to the doctor. Based on your wishes, taking into account the specific physiological data, the specialist will tell you how you can realize your expectations, what effect will be achieved at the end and how long it will last, and also will warn about possible consequences.
