Become the Master of Aptos!

Date 26 October 2020
Location Online training

It has been about 7 months we are forced to live in an unusual environment. Since that we have conducted more than hundred webinars and online training courses for more than 10 thousand doctors across the world. We have taught about our products individually, covered methods, zones and approaches, but in order to convert education into systematic knowledge, we are moving to use a different approach. With that in mind, our scientific and education unit designed a special teaching scheme. 

 

We are thrilled to launch an astounding educational initiative, series of comprehensive thread lift training courses - Master of Aptos. The series will deliver knowledge about Aptos methods, as well as cover the full range of information on Aptos combinations portfolio - the approaches and mechanics. The Masterclass will be led by the inventors, experts, and global scale opinion leaders.

 

Starting from November 12th and until December 3rd (throughout 4 weeks) we will deliver 4 independent training courses and 8 hours of educational content about upper, middle, and lower face, followed by a comprehensive study of nose lift with threads.

 

Every training session will start with anatomy masterclass led by Prof. Sebastian Cotofana (USA), followed by Dr. George Sulamanidze (Georgia) and Dr. Albina Kajaia (Georgia). In the frame of 2 hours session, the speakers will present 2 sets of combinations complimenting theory with practical demonstrations. After those 3 training courses, the nose lift masterclass will be held by Dr. Marlen Sulamanidze (Georgia) and Dr. Konstantin Sulamanidze (Georgia) together with Dr. Yves Saban (France).

 

The Aptos doctors who will attend all 4 the masterclasses, will get not just the certificate but a special custom-tailored surgical hat – Master of Aptos. Most importantly, the series are developed to share the priceless Knowledge that cannot be acquired anywhere else.
