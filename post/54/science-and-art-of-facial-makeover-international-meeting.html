Science and Art of facial Makeover International Meeting

Date 20 April 2018
Location Beirut, Lebanon

Have a look at our booth on Science and Art of facial Makeover International Meeting held in Phoenicia Hotel, Beirut, Lebanon on April 18,19 and 20.

﻿Facial makeover combines many specialties who work on the entire face, this meeting aims to bring all aspect of wide range of expertise together to generate interaction between specialist from all disciplines, to share, teach and learn.

During this unique event, internationally renowned Facial Plastic and Maxillofacial Surgeons will be joined by colleagues in Maxillofacial Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Otolaryngology & Dermatology in discussing the nuances of Facial Makeover.

The meeting features the latest trends and technologies in facial plastic and maxillofacial surgery by offering lectures, panels, instructional courses led by international experts in the field, and an exhibition of surgical & pharmaceutical companies displaying and introducing their latest products and services.

The educational offer will be to learn from those who have more experience and listen to those who are the future “learning everything from everyone”: We know that to travel far, there is no better way than knowledge.
