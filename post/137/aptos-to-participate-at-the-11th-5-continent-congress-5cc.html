Aptos to participate at the 11th 5-Continent-Congress (5CC)

Date 24 July 2019
Location CCIB - Centre de Convencions Internacional de Barcelona (Spain)

We are happy to announce our participation in the 11th 5-Continent-Congress (5CC), August 29 – September 1, 2019 in Barcelona. 


The 5-Continent-Congress is comprised of an international group of highly acclaimed dermatologists and plastic surgeons who are key opinion leaders in their fields. Since the first edition in 2009, the 5CC has been recognized internationally as one of the top conferences dedicated to innovations and controversies in the field of medical and aesthetic dermatology. In four days and 65+ sessions, the scientific program covers all the many facets of medical dermatology; injectables for aesthetic and medical use; cosmetic and aesthetic energy-based devices; aesthetic gynecology; practice management and digital marketing.


Please view the 5CC registration page for more information.
