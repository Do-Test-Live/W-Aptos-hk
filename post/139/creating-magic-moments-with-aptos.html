Creating Magic Moments with Aptos

Date 8 July 2019
Location Tbilisi, Georgia

On behalf of the whole Aptos, let me express our heartfelt gratitude to all of trainers and experts for joining us for the ‘Creating Magic of Aptos Workshop’ earlier this month in Tbilisi.

 

It has been our absolute privilege and honor to welcome you - great professionals with extraordinary experiences and skills - to share knowledge, learn new things and get connected as One Family! We are hopeful that sharing insights and your own Formulas of Success was an unforgettable experience, and helped learn more about each other’s values and passions. Diving deep into discussions about the future of Aptos and the role of Brand Ambassadors gave us an idea of global community – a community that drives the industry of thread lifting and delivers the best possible outcome. 

 

As promised, this event was not ‘just another meeting or a conference’ but rather a sparkling moment to celebrate our success and achievements by sharing your amazing personal stories. Later next month we promise to share with you a meeting report and a book - ‘A Collection of 50 Stories – My Success Formula’, which will be a compilation of your views on success and motivation. We hope this little book will become a good commemoration of the day we spent together, full of emotional coziness, and will demonstrate the pride and passion we share in our business. 

 

Thanks much again for all your positive vibes, enthusiasm and great energy. We are looking forward to sharing many more of such experiences and emotions with you in the years to come.  

 

Let’s keep creating magic!
