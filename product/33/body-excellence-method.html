Body EXCELLENCE METHOD
Soft Blunt HA

Body Excellence is resorbable thread with multidirectional, specially designed barbs. The unique feature enhances the force of body tissue holding. Multidirectional barbs can distribute stress and hold soft tissues stronger after insertion.

Body Excellence can be used for approximation and rejuvenation of various parts of the body: arms, breast, abdomen, buttocks, inner thighs, knees.

Efficient design of Body Excellence fixates the entire treatment area through an easy and noninvasive procedure.

Threads are inserted through atraumatic, blunt tip cannula thus avoiding extra bleeding and swelling after the procedure.

The method is easy and fast to perform and needs practically no downtime. As a result, the skin is redefined, volume restored and the effects of ageing are significantly reduced.

Blunt tip needle 19Gx200mm, straight - 2pcs
