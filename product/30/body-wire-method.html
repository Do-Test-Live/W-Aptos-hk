Body WIRE METHOD
Soft Blunt HA

Wire Method - Aptos wire method is a wire tool with a straight needle.

With Wire Method it is possible to pass the needle through hypodermic tissues on a closed contour without skin incisions. The method can be used in the general plastic and aesthetic surgery.

Aptos Wire Method removes wrinkles and folds atrophic and indrawn scars from the face and body, as well as unevenness of the skin and subcutaneous tissues resulted from an unsuccessful liposuction. The manipulation is carried out without incisions, only through pin punctures, without any filler, using a specially designed device.

In Pack:

Wire Method

Stainless steel wire USP 3/0, EP 2, 450mm. - 2pcs.

Trocar point needle 0.8x85mm. - 1pcs.

Standard needle attachment

Application Areas
Glabellar lines
Forehead wrinkles
Chin fold
Nasolabial fold
